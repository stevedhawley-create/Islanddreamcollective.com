What we do

Everything. So you don't have to.

From listing optimisation to on-ground maintenance — IDC manages every detail of your villa so it performs at its best while you stay hands-free.

Full service management

One company. Everything covered.

Most villa management companies handle bookings and not much else. IDC manages the complete picture — every touchpoint from listing to checkout, with real transparency on how your property is performing.

📋

Listing Management

We manage your listings across Airbnb, Booking.com and VRBO — professional photography, optimised descriptions, and dynamic pricing that responds to market conditions. Plus direct bookings via balivillabookings.com with zero OTA commission.

💬

Guest Communication

Every enquiry, every message, every review — handled promptly and professionally. We maintain response times that keep your listing rankings high and guests happy.

🏡

Property Operations

Housekeeping coordination, pre-arrival preparation, check-ins and check-outs. We treat your villa like it's our own — because our reputation depends on it.

🔧

Maintenance & Repairs

Our on-ground team handles routine maintenance and coordinates repairs with trusted local suppliers. Issues are resolved quickly, costs are transparent, and nothing falls through the cracks.

📊

Owner Reporting

Your own live dashboard showing bookings, revenue, expenses and occupancy in real time. Monthly summaries and always-on access — no waiting for a PDF once a month.

💰

Revenue Optimisation

Dynamic pricing, seasonal strategy, platform-specific optimisation and direct booking incentives. We actively manage your revenue — not just report on it.

📸

Photography & Presentation

Professional villa photography for listings and marketing. First impressions drive bookings — we make sure yours are exceptional.

🌿

Villa Styling & Upkeep

Regular villa inspections, garden and pool maintenance coordination, restocking of supplies. Your villa stays guest-ready at all times.
